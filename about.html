About Me

Hello World. I was born in Mexico City on October 16th, 1973. I grew up in Los Angeles, CA, but now live in San Bernardino, CA. I've always had a passion for art and design since a young kid. I moved back to Mexico City in my early 20's where I studied Graphic Design and Illustration at Universidad Tecnologica de Mexico (UNITEC).

After graduating college, I moved back to So. California where I planned to pursue a career in webpage design. Unfortunately, I switched careers to automobile sales which lasted about 18 years.

I started learning Web Develpment in early 2020 with an online course in Udemy by Colt Steel. Later in May of 2021 I enrolled in a Software Engineer Bootcamp at University of California Riverside which I am currently enrolled.
